Fancy Cubed Ice

Description

A real crowd pleaser! This recipe will serve you for years, and although a bit difficult, with patience will yield a perfect product.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups water (approximately).
  • 2 tablespoons water (additional if needed).
  • Ice trays with which to set the product.

Preparation:

  1. Empty the ice cubes that are left in the trays (if any left) into the sink. Ice will spoil if left longer than 3 days.
  2. Briskly mix in the two tablespoons of water into the 2 cups in a large mixing bowl. Stir until the 2 are completely mixed.
  3. Gently pour the mixture into the trays, Evenly! If this is not carefully done the two components could separate.
  4. Set the tray in your freezer and check every 20 minutes until hardened.

Authors' Notes:

Make 100% sure you employ the freezer for this recipe. The refridgerator sadly, just results in a very runny product.
